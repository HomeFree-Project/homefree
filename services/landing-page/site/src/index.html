The product

One box that sits between you and the internet — and runs everything you'd otherwise rent.

Most self-hosting projects are app launchers. HomeFree is also your router: dynamic DNS, automatic HTTPS, firewall, ad-blocking DNS, mesh VPN — built in.

A curated, growing catalog of apps comes pre-wired behind a single sign-on, on hardware you own, on a network you control. Sign in once with HomeFree SSO and every app trusts you.

{# ───────────────────────── Feature pillars ─────────────────── #}
Why one box

Sovereign by default. Boring to operate.

You shouldn't have to assemble a router, a domain, your apps, and half-a-dozen accounts to get a working personal cloud. HomeFree gives you all of it as a single, friendly system.

Real router, not just apps

A real home network in one box: firewall, ad-blocker, automatic HTTPS, a private VPN so your phone reaches your apps from anywhere. No fiddly setup. No second device to plug in.

One sign-in, every app

Your photos, files, chat, calendar, password vault — all behind a single login. Add a family member once and they have access to everything they should, nowhere they shouldn't.

Boring to run, easy to fix

A guided installer gets you up in an afternoon. Updates are one click. If something breaks, rolling back is one click too. You will not be living in a terminal.

Your hardware, your data

Bring your own mini PC or buy one preloaded. Backups are encrypted and yours — to a local disk, a NAS, or any S3-compatible provider. Walk away with a USB drive any day.

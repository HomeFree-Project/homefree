# HomeFree Manual

Welcome. HomeFree is a small computer that lives in your house and quietly runs your own cloud — your files, your photos, your calendars, your chat, your news, all of it on hardware you own.

This manual walks you through three things:

1. **Setting up the hardware** — what to buy, how to plug it in.
2. **Installing HomeFree** — a guided installer on a USB stick.
3. **Using the apps** — page by page, the apps you can run and how to connect your phone or laptop to them.

If you can plug in a router, you can do this. Most people finish in an afternoon.
